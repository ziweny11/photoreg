PhotoReg: Photometrically Registering 3D Gaussian Splatting Models

Ziwen Yuan1, 1Carnegie Mellon University
Paper arXiv Video

Abstract

Building accurate representations of the environment is critical for intelligent robots to make decisions during deployment. This paper presents PhotoReg, a framework for photometrically registering 3D Gaussian Splatting models using photometric losses and 3D foundation models.
